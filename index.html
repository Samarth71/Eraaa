About How it Works Eranatis ↗

About

We're an AI writing company with a goal of creating a unified, polymathic writing system — one that solves the fragmentation problem in writing AI tools. We're based in Gondia, Maharashtra, India.

The Fragmentation Problem

The bottleneck in AI writing tools isn't capability — it's the fragmented nature of currently available apps, each designed for specific use cases and narrow workflows. This deployment fragmentation leads to a world where you have different tools for essays, emails, captions, stories, and summaries.

We realized that breadth-oriented tools can solve the fragmentation problem in ways narrow ones never could. Eranatis — the broadest AI writing tool built to understand all writing needs — is our closest-yet expression of that insight.

Eranatis' thinking framework

One system, all writing needs

Instead of switching between 5 different apps, Eranatis reasons across all writing modalities — essays, emails, captions, stories, poems, summaries, and more. One system that understands what you need and delivers it.

Language-aware generation

Most AI tools assume English. Eranatis is built natively for India — supporting English, Hindi, and Hinglish. Because your best ideas come in the language you think in, not the one you're forced to write in.

Tone-aware, not template-based

Eranatis doesn't fill in blanks. It actually reasons about what you need — Professional, Casual, Funny, Formal, or Simple — and writes with genuine understanding of context, audience, and purpose.

Intelligence, not automation

We don't believe in copy-paste templates or robotic outputs. Every generation is reasoned from scratch — understanding your topic, your audience, your language, and producing something that actually communicates.

Who's behind this

Samarth Asati — Founder. I'm 14, and I'm from Gondia, Maharashtra. I'm an engineer, thinker, and a writer. I started Eranalum as a series of experiments — exploring how AI could make writing more accessible for every Indian student and creator, regardless of the language they think in. I noticed that every AI writing tool was either too narrow, too English-centric, or too expensive for the average Indian user. Eranatis is my answer to that — a unified, multilingual writing intelligence built for India, by someone who grew up here.

How it Works

Eranatis is simple to use — but powerful underneath. Here's exactly how it works.

01

Choose your writing mode

Select from 8 modes — Essay, Email, Story, Caption, Poem, Improve, Summarize, or Translate. Each mode tells Eranatis what kind of output you need.

02

Pick your language & tone

Choose English, Hindi, or Hinglish. Then pick a tone — Professional, Casual, Funny, Formal, or Simple. Eranatis adapts its entire output to match your selection.

03

Type your topic or paste your text

Describe what you want — or paste existing text if you want to improve or summarize. You can also attach a .txt or .pdf file for extra context.

04

Generate, copy or download

Hit generate — Eranatis writes in seconds. Copy to clipboard or download as a .txt file. Not happy? Hit Regenerate for a fresh take.
